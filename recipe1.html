Pasta Recipe

Home Pasta Burger Pizza Cake Salad

Preparation Time: 30 minutes

Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 2 cups pasta
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup cheese
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Boil water and cook pasta for 8-10 minutes.
  2. Drain the pasta and keep aside.
  3. Heat oil in a pan and add tomato sauce.
  4. Add cooked pasta and mix well.
  5. Add cheese and salt.
  6. Cook for 2-3 minutes and serve hot.
