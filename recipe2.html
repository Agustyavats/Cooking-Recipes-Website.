Burger Recipe

Home Pasta Burger Pizza Cake Salad

Preparation Time: 20 minutes

Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 2 burger buns
  • 1 veg or chicken patty
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Tomato slices
  • Cheese slice
  • Mayonnaise
  • Ketchup

Steps

  1. Cut the burger buns into halves.
  2. Toast the buns lightly on a pan.
  3. Cook the patty until golden brown.
  4. Spread mayonnaise and ketchup on buns.
  5. Add lettuce, tomato, patty, and cheese.
  6. Cover with top bun and serve.
