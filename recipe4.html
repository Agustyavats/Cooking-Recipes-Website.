Cake Recipe

Home Pasta Burger Pizza Cake Salad

Preparation Time: 60 minutes

Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • Vanilla essence

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C.
  2. Mix flour and baking powder.
  3. Beat butter and sugar until smooth.
  4. Add eggs and mix well.
  5. Add milk and flour mixture.
  6. Pour into baking tray.
  7. Bake for 30-35 minutes.
  8. Cool and serve.
