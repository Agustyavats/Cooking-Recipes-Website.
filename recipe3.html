Pizza Recipe

Home Pasta Burger Pizza Cake Salad

Preparation Time: 40 minutes

Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients

  • Pizza base
  • Tomato sauce
  • Cheese
  • Capsicum
  • Onion
  • Olives
  • Oregano

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C.
  2. Spread tomato sauce on pizza base.
  3. Add cheese evenly.
  4. Add vegetables and toppings.
  5. Bake for 15-20 minutes.
  6. Sprinkle oregano and serve hot.
