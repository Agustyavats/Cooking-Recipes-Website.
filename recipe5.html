Preparation Time: 15 minutes

Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • Cucumber
  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • Lettuce
  • Olives
  • Salt
  • Lemon juice

Steps

  1. Wash and chop all vegetables.
  2. Add them to a bowl.
  3. Add salt and lemon juice.
  4. Mix well.
  5. Serve fresh.
